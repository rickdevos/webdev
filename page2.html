24 November 2020

Everyday Life nominated for Album of the Year GRAMMY

Good evening. We're very pleased to report that the band's Everyday Life Album has been nominated in two categories at the 63rd Annual GRAMMY Awards. The nominations are:

- Album of the year

- Best Recording Package (NB: You can download the album booklet Here)

The Winners will be announced at the ceremony on sunday 31 January 2021.
